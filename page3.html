Interested in how we made Eli?

Eli the Elf was made using state of the art technology. With a mixture of hand crafted sewing and next generation 3D printing (and a little bit of magic) we were able to create the doll. As for the human form, we simply imbued Eli with a life force that was fully activatable with a simple voice command that is completely customizeable and synced to your voice. Eli's command centre is controlled with a small panel in his back, here you can change and set your voice command.
